About

Hello there.

I am Jiaqi Feng, you can also call me George. Over the past eight years, I have been investing my time in software engineering. I taught myself C# when I was in middle school and started writing utility applications using WPF and Windows Forms. After I got into high school, I started writing applications for Windows Phone, and learning Xamarin so that I could implement my ideas onto more platforms. These technologies are more than obsolete, but still left me great amount of experience in software design and engineering. Currently, as a college student, my enthusiasm for designing software has not waned at all. I have been developing applications that are useful for both iOS and Android, of which many has already been released to app store. That said, I have more hands-on experience than most of my contemporaries and therefore would adapt to professional environment more easily.

My Personal Projects

Listed below are some personal projects I have been doing which I am very proud of. Click on the image then you will be navigated to the store page.

CoronaCounter

A simple utility application for Mac OS to display the statistics of Coronavirus of selected country on status bar.

Manji - Kanji Study Made Easy

Manji is an iOS application designed to make kanji learning experience much easier by organzing kanji into different catogories and allowing users to design and track their customized learning objectives.

Kaby - Easy Task Management

Kaby is a todo app that takes advantage of the benefits of kanban, dividing your tasks into three categories: todo, doing and done, making your personal task management easier and smoother than ever.

Dumbbell - Workout in your way

Unlike most workout apps which provides users with a variety of pre-designed workout routines, Dumbbell is an iOS application that allows users to design their own workout routines and track their progress.

Journalyst

Journalyst is an simple yet elegant iOS journal app.
